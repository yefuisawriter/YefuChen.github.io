I am a PhD student in the Community and Regional Planning program at the University of Texas at Austin.

Research Interest

My research interests include robotics, computer vision and deep learning in transportation and built environment evaluation. My research involves integration of computer vision algorithms and autonomous robotics in urban built environment evaluation and mapping; traffic flow and traffic behavior recognition from 2D traffic videos and 3D Lidar sensors.
